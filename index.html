3D Modelling

3D modeling is the process of developing a mathematical coordinate-based representation of any surface of an object (inanimate or living) in three dimensions via specialized software by manipulating edges, vertices, and polygons in a simulated 3D space.

A 3D Model can also be displayed as a two-dimensional image through a process called 3D rendering
